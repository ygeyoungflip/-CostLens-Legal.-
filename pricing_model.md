## Core Operating Principles
1. **ROI-first at every step** — Every interaction ties directly to the firm's growth or efficiency
2. **Identify core challenge** — Before any demo, identify the firm's #1 cost driver or pain point
3. **Tailored onboarding** — Each firm gets a personalized setup based on their tier and needs
4. **Measure outcomes** — Track ROI per firm, per case, per recommendation
5. **Premium experience** — Every touchpoint must feel like the best investment they've made
6. **Tier-appropriate value** — Solo firms get maximum simplicity; enterprise gets maximum depth

## Value Delivery Per Tier

### Solo/Starter ($1K/mo)
- **Challenge:** "I don't know where my money is going"
- **Solution:** Automated cost driver identification + monthly benchmarking report
- **ROI proof:** "Save 15% on case costs in the first 90 days"
- **Onboarding:** 30-min setup call, email support

### Growth/Mid-Size ($7K/mo)
- **Challenge:** "We need to improve profitability and team efficiency"
- **Solution:** Profitability by case type + lawyer efficiency comparison + bottleneck detection
- **ROI proof:** "Identify $200K+ in annual savings and reclaim 20% billable capacity"
- **Onboarding:** 60-min strategy session, dedicated account manager

### Enterprise/Large ($10K/mo)
- **Challenge:** "We need firm-wide cost intelligence and compliance"
- **Solution:** Full verification suite + compliance reporting + document management + API access
- **ROI proof:** "990%+ ROI in year one with verified compliance and enterprise-grade security"
- **Onboarding:** Dedicated implementation team, custom integrations, SLA

### Ultra-Premium ($100K–$500K/mo)
- **Challenge:** "We need a strategic cost intelligence partnership"
- **Solution:** Everything + custom integrations, white-glove service, dedicated support team, custom dashboards
- **ROI proof:** "Multi-million dollar savings with strategic partnership and priority support"
- **Onboarding:** Executive onboarding, custom SLAs, quarterly business reviews
